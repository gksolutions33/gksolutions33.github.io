Who We Are

GK Solutions is a blog and project hub for tech enthusiasts, students, and DIY makers. We specialize in hands-on tutorials related to:

  • Embedded systems (Arduino, SIM808, sensors)
  • Python programming
  • IoT and automation
  • Artificial Intelligence basics

Our Mission

Our goal is to make complex technologies easy to understand by breaking them down into simple, step-by-step guides. Whether you're a beginner or a developer, we have something for you.
